--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
MATHEMATICAL FRAMEWORK
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Hierarchical:    σ²_total = σ²_raw + σ²_epistemic + σ²_aleatoric

Ensemble:        σ²_total = E[σ²_within] + Var[μ_between]

Conformal:       P(y ∈ C(x)) ≥ 1-α  (distribution-free guarantee)

Second-Order:    CV = σ[Var] / E[Var]  (unreliable if CV > 0.3)
